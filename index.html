Housing Affordability Analysis Across U.S. States

A state-level analysis that compares median household income, home price growth, and affordability patterns across the United States using Python, Census data, and FHFA housing data.

This project focused on identifying which states appeared more affordable for first-time homebuyers and showing how affordability changes when income and housing trends are looked at together.
