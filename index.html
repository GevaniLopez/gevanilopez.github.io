Retention Analytics

Customer Retention and Revenue Performance Analysis

A business-focused ecommerce case study using Google BigQuery, SQL, Python, and Jupyter to analyze one-time versus repeat customer behavior, revenue mix, and second-order conversion.

This project examines how dependent the business is on first-time buyers, how much revenue comes from repeat orders, and which product categories appear slightly more associated with customers returning for a second purchase. The analysis focuses on practical retention opportunities tied to long-term revenue efficiency rather than just describing purchase patterns.
