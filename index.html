About Me

I created this portfolio to show how I approach real data problems from start to finish. That includes preparing messy data, exploring patterns, creating visualizations, and interpreting results honestly instead of forcing conclusions.

My background includes Python, SQL, dashboards, and project-based analytics work. As I continue building this site, I want it to reflect both my technical skills and the way I think through problems in a practical, grounded way.

Business Analysis

Housing Affordability for First-Time Homebuyers Across U.S. States

A state-level analysis that combines median household income, FHFA housing data, and recent home price growth to evaluate where first-time homeownership appears more or less affordable across the United States.

This project focuses on using multiple indicators together instead of relying on home prices alone, helping create a clearer picture of affordability patterns and how they may affect decision-making.

Customer Segmentation for Targeted Marketing Strategy

Built a customer segmentation model using e-commerce order, payment, review, and product data to identify six customer groups and translate them into targeted marketing recommendations.

This project focused on finding meaningful customer groups based on how people purchase, review, and interact with the business. From there, I turned those groups into practical recommendations and estimated the revenue upside of improving repeat-purchase behavior in the strongest one-time shopper segments.
